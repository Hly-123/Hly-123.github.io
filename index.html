Hui Zhang

I am currently a PhD student at ETH Zurich, supervised by Prof. Mirko Meboldt. Prior to this, I work as a research assistant at AIT lab, ETH Zurich, supervised by Prof. Otmar Hilliges, with the topic of physically plausible human motion generation.

I received my master's degree in Robotics, System & Control at ETH Zurich. During my master, I completed my master thesis at Robotic Systems Lab, ETH Zurich, supervised by Prof. Marco Hutter and my semester project at Computational Robotics Lab, ETH Zurich, supervised by Prof. Stelian Coros. Previously, I got my bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University, with my thesis completed in Langer Lab, MIT.

Currently, my research interests lie in physics-based hand-object interaction, dexterous robotic hand manipulation, and their applications in manufacturing, such as assembly error detection.
